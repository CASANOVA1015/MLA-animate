Eigenspace Decomposition: Understanding γ through its eigenvalues and eigenvectors.

🔴 Eigenvalue λ = 0 (Kernel)

• Eigenvector: 1 = (1,1)

• This is the direction that gets "collapsed" to zero

• γ·1 = 0·1 = 0

• Represents the "mean direction" that we remove

🔵 Eigenvalue λ = 1 (Image)

• All vectors in 1⊥

• These directions are preserved

• γ·v = 1·v = v for all v ⊥ 1

• This is the subspace where centered data lives
